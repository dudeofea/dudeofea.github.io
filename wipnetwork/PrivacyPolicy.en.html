Confidentiality & Privacy Policy

What we discuss will be held in confidence and not shared with others. However, our discussions are not privileged for legal purposes. The privilege of confidentiality that exists for the clergy, therapists and attorneys is not granted to coaching professionals. That said, life coaching is a confidential process, and your personal information will always be protected.  Work in Progress coaching provides equality of services and care to everyone regardless of people's age, ability, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, race, religion or belief or sexual orientation.
